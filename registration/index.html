Registration

Please use this form to register for the Dutch Cerebellum Day 2024.
Note that registration is free for all participants and includes a full day of cerebellar research, coffee, snacks, lunch and drinks at the end of the day.

! We ask you to register as soon as possible, so we have an indication how many people will be attending.


Posters: if you would like to present a poster during this day, please indicate so on the registration form. Please do so before the 1st of May. All posters should be in ‘Portrait’ orientation, size A0. Posters are presented from 13:20-14:30 and are removed at the end of the day (after 18:00).
